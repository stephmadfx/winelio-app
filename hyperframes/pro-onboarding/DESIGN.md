# Winelio Pro Onboarding Video

## Style Prompt

Modern, clean French SaaS onboarding video for Winelio professionals. The video should feel warm, credible, and energetic: professional enough for business users, simple enough to understand in under one minute. Use Winelio orange/amber accents, a light canvas, dark readable typography, soft panels, subtle network lines, and crisp motion.

## Colors

- Brand orange: #FF6B35
- Brand amber: #F7931E
- Dark text: #2D3436
- Gray text: #636E72
- Light background: #F8F9FA
- Soft accent background: #FFF5F0
- Success green: #16A34A

## Typography

- Primary: Poppins, Montserrat, Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif
- Use large, direct French headlines and compact supporting text.

## Motion

- Smooth, confident motion with short entrances.
- Use staggered reveals, line drawing, progress steps, and gentle scale accents.
- Keep every text block readable long enough for French viewers.

## What NOT to Do

- No purple or blue SaaS gradients.
- No dense legal copy.
- No tiny text.
- No dark, blurry, or stock-like visuals.
- No chaotic motion or overly playful effects.
